*Last updated: 21 Nov 2025*

## Controller & contact
Detector de IA (individually operated). Email: support@detectordeia.pro

## Data we process
- **Account**: email, optional name, hashed credentials, language/theme prefs.
- **Service use**: texts/files/URLs you submit, scores/results, history kept 90 days.
- **Technical**: IP, user agent, product events (GA4, Clarity, Plausible).
- **Billing**: minimal data to process payments via Creem (no card storage by us).

## Purposes & legal bases
Service delivery, security, product metrics (consent for analytics cookies), billing and support.

## Processors & storage
Supabase (Postgres), Cloudflare R2 (files), GA4, Clarity, self-hosted Plausible, Creem for payments.

## Retention
History 90 days; logs up to 90 days; account data until deletion.

## We do NOT
Sell data; train models with your content; share beyond listed providers.

## Your rights
Access, rectify, erase, object, port. Email support@detectordeia.pro. EU/EEA users may contact their local DPA.

## International transfers
Data may be processed in providers' locations (e.g., US); standard safeguards apply where required.

## Minors
No specific age gate; service intended for users able to contract. Guardians may request deletion for minors.

## Changes
We will update this page; material changes will be announced in-site.
