*Last updated: 21 Nov 2025*

## Quick summary
- Controller: Detector de IA (individually operated). Contact: support@detectordeia.pro
- **Essential cookies** for login, preferences, security.
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity, self-hosted Plausible.
- No marketing/advertising cookies today; no data selling. If we later enable ads (e.g., Google AdSense), we will treat them as marketing cookies and ask for explicit consent before loading.
- Consent banner lets you accept/reject analytics anytime.

## What we use
- **Strictly necessary**: session (`better-auth`), language/theme, CSRF protection.
- **Analytics (consent)**: GA4 for traffic, Clarity for heatmaps, Plausible for aggregated metrics (aimed to be cookieless).

## Managing consent
Change your choice in the banner or clear cookies in your browser.

## Retention
GA4 up to 14 months; Clarity up to 12 months; Plausible aggregated; essentials up to 1 year (language/theme), session while logged in.

## Third parties
Google LLC; Microsoft Corporation; self-hosted Plausible under our control.

## Changes
We will post updates here; material changes will be highlighted in-site.
