*Last updated: 21 Nov 2025*

## Controller & contact
Detector de IA (individually operated). Email: support@detectordeia.pro

## Data we process
- **Account**: email, optional name, hashed credentials, language/theme prefs.
- **Service use**: texts/files/URLs you submit, scores/results; history you can choose to keep 0, 30 or 90 days (90 by default if you don’t choose).
- **Technical**: IP, user agent, product events (GA4, Clarity, self-hosted Plausible).
- **Billing**: minimal data to process payments via Creem (we don’t store card numbers).

## Purposes & legal bases
- Service delivery and showing history (contract).
- Authentication & security (legitimate interest).
- Product metrics and improvement: GA4 and Clarity based on consent; Plausible aggregated/self-hosted (legitimate interest).
- Billing and support (contract).

## Processors & storage
Supabase (Postgres), Cloudflare R2 (files), GA4, Clarity, self-hosted Plausible, Google/GitHub OAuth, Creem for payments, Resend for transactional/support email.

## Retention
History configurable by you: 0 / 30 / 90 days (90 default). Technical logs up to 90 days. Account data until deletion.

## We do NOT
Sell data; train models with your content; share beyond listed providers.

## Your rights
Access, rectify, erase, object, port. Email support@detectordeia.pro. EU/EEA users may contact their local DPA.

## International transfers
Data may be processed in providers' locations (e.g., US); standard safeguards apply where required.

## Minors
No specific age gate, but intended for users able to contract. Guardians may request deletion for minors.

## Changes
We will update this page; material changes will be announced in-site.

## U.S. state privacy (CCPA/CPRA)
We do not “sell” or “share” personal data. Based on current scale we don’t meet CPRA applicability thresholds; if that changes we will provide a “Do Not Sell/Share” mechanism and honor global privacy signals (GPC).
